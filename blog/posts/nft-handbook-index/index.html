Introduction to NFTs

An NFT is a digital asset that is non-fungible by nature. NFTs can exist in a variety of forms: art, music, videos, etc. NFTs will revolutionize the ownership of assets. Why not surprise your friends and family with an NFT as a gift? Why not convert your own unique artwork into an NFT and put it up for auction? From creating your music and selling it as an NFT to capturing photos and transforming them into digital assets, so much can be done under one roof.

To get on board, you just have to go to NFT marketplaces and start creating and selling your NFTs.
