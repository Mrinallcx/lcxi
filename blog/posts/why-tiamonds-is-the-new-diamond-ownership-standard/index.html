Tiamonds.com enables users to acquire a diamond that is both dazzling and technologically advanced, a piece of history. The process is as follows:

Own Real Diamonds, Not Just Pixels

The capability to possess genuine, tangible diamonds is one of the basic features of Tiamonds.com. Every Tiamond acquired is accompanied by a meticulously selected natural diamond: 1:1. A high-security vault in Liechtenstein ensures the safety and integrity of these gemstones.

This methodology dismantles traditional barriers to diamond ownership. Tiamond holders are not merely the proprietor of a digital representation with Tiamonds.com; they are the legitimate owner of a tangible, physical asset that is backed by blockchain technology.

Ethical Sourcing: Diamonds One Can Feel Good About

Tiamonds.com places a high value on responsible sourcing practices. We ensure the ethical and conflict-free sourcing of all our diamonds. We adhere to the Kimberley Process Certification Scheme (KPCS), a United Nations effort that oversees the trade in rough diamonds in order to avoid conflict diamonds from accessing the legitimate market.

Our diamonds adhere to the most stringent ethical standards, ensuring that the investment is in accordance with social responsibility.

Peace of Mind and Flexibility: Redeem Diamonds Anytime

Tiamonds.com provides an indispensable advantage: redemption flexibility. Holders have the option to retain the Tiamond as a digital asset or exchange it for a tangible diamond at their discretion. The procedure is uncomplicated. The NFT is converted into a digital memento, which serves as a permanent record of their ownership, upon redemption of their Tiamond.

This feature accommodates diverse investor preferences. Some individuals may prefer to retain their diamonds for the purpose of long-term appreciation, while others may desire the physical attractiveness of owning a stunning diamond. Tiamonds.com offers the flexibility to make a selection.

Ownership Redefined: NFT Ownership Deed

Tiamonds.com revolutionizes diamond ownership by leveraging the power of non-fungible tokens (NFTs). An NFT serves as a digital ownership deed for each Tiamond. This NFT functions as a tamper-proof record of their legal proprietorship of both the digital and physical asset.

Blockchain technology guarantees the immutability and transparency of the NFT. This novel method simplifies record-keeping and improves the overall experience by offering a secure and reliable method of tracking diamond ownership.

Confidence in Authenticity: GIA & LCX Certification

Two critical certifications are included with each Tiamond:

GIA Certification: The Gemological Institute of America (GIA), the world’s foremost authority on diamond grading, conducts a rigorous evaluation procedure for each diamond. The GIA certificate offers a comprehensive report that delineates the diamond’s attributes, such as its color, clarity, carat weight, and cut. This guarantees transparency and provides holders with valuable information regarding the quality of their diamond.

LCX Certification: The LCX is essential for the tokenization procedure. The tangible diamond asset associated with their Tiamond is authenticated and verified by LCX. This supplementary layer of security serves to strengthen their confidence in the integrity of their investment.

Secure Storage: Diamond Under Protection

Security is one of the most significant concerns when investing in physical assets. Tiamonds.com provides secure vault storage for their diamonds. We partner with a high-security facility in Liechtenstein, renowned for its stringent security protocols. Furthermore, Lloyd’s of London, the world’s foremost insurance marketplace, provides additional protection for users investment.

This comprehensive security strategy guarantees peace of mind. One can rest assured that Lloyd’s of London’s insurance expertise and state-of-the-art facility will protect their diamond.
