Tiamonds proudly participated in Davos 2025, joining the global discussion on tokenization and blockchain innovation. This prestigious event provided the perfect platform to demonstrate our groundbreaking advancements in tokenized diamonds and beyond. Here is a glimpse into our exciting journey at the World Economic Forum.

Magnificent Booth and a Chance to Win a Diamond

Our first day at Davos was nothing short of spectacular. We welcomed attendees to our stunning booth, showcasing our commitment to innovation and excellence. Visitors were not just amazed by the tokenized diamonds on display but also had a unique opportunity to win one! The chance to own a real tokenized diamond added an interactive and thrilling element to our presence. This initiative highlighted our dedication to making tokenization more engaging and accessible to everyone.

Redefining Asset Tokenization with TOTO Token

Moreover, we are thrilled to introduce a major milestone in our journey: the rebranding of the TIA token to the TOTO token, symbolizing “Total Tokenization.” This upgrade marks a transformative shift as we expand beyond diamonds to tokenize a diverse range of physical and financial assets. From precious metals like gold, silver, and platinum to gemstones such as sapphires and rubies, and even real estate and other inaccessible assets, $TOTO is unlocking endless possibilities. Our vision is to give you freedom of ownership by making it more secure, tradable, and accessible.
