How to Redeem Physical Diamonds?

Tiamond owners can redeem their Tiamond and physically receive their diamond. In order to get the diamond and keep it in your possession, you need to follow a few steps.

  • You must have a fully verified LCX account. 
  • Contact our support at hello@LCX.com. 
  • We will send you an email with the shipping costs and service fee total for redemption, along with instructions to pay these fees and a wallet address to send the Tiamond NFT token to. 
  • After we have received your payment and Tiamond, we will take your diamond from our vault and mail it to the address you specified, or you may also pick it up in Liechtenstein personally.

About Polytrade

About Tiamonds

Tiamonds is the largest platform worldwide for tokenized diamonds that are fully asset-backed with $TIA tokens as rewards. These are certified diamonds that are stored securely in a vault in Liechtenstein. It guarantees the authenticity and security of these priceless assets through insurance by Lloyd’s London and certification by LCX and GIA. Tiamonds has partnered with LCX as a Physical Validator, ensuring that the value of the tokenized diamonds is backed by natural, high-quality diamonds. Additionally, each holder has the option to redeem the physical diamond.
