Paris Blockchain Week 2025 – Highlights & Takeaways from Last Week

April 15, 2025 - 3 min read

Tiamonds at PBW 2025: Tokenizing the Tangible – Legally, Transparently, On-Chain

We had an incredible time at Paris Blockchain Week 2025, where Tiamonds showcased its vision for the future of tokenized real-world assets (RWA). The event was packed with networking, partnerships, and meaningful discussions that continue to push the boundaries of blockchain innovation.

Tokenized Sapphire Collection Launch 

One of the standout moments at PBW was the launch of the world’s first-ever tokenized sapphire collection. This was a significant milestone for Tiamonds, and we couldn’t have asked for a better partner than gemstone experts Berr & Partners.

We presented a unique tokenized sapphire collection & the event kicked off with a high-quality panel, introduced by the renowned Don Tapscott (Chairman of the Blockchain Research Institute), which attracted some of the brightest minds in the blockchain space.

The turnout was fantastic, with great engagement and meaningful conversations about the future of asset-backed tokenization. 

Strategic Partner Meetings & Ecosystem Engagement

We kicked off Paris Blockchain Week with numerous strategic meetings. Our discussions with both existing and new partners were highly productive, sparking collaborations with blockchains and protocols interested in Real World Assets (RWA) and the Tiamonds model.

Our ability to integrate with various blockchains and offer real-world value through tokenization was a key point of interest. The week began with several exciting collaborations aligned with Tiamonds’ mission.

Thought Leadership & Industry Visibility

Tiamonds was front and center at PBW, attending curated side events and exclusive VIP gatherings. We were also part of an engaging panel discussion on “The truth behind Tokenization and RWAs”, which garnered much attention.

Our discussions highlighted Tiamonds’ unique selling points (USPs), including our first-mover advantage in the RWA space. Tiamonds is the largest diamond tokenization platform worldwide, with over 25,000 investable diamonds available. We have established a strong legal framework since 2023, launched Tokenized Gold bars, and are now expanding into Tokenized Sapphires. All assets on the platform are redeemable, further reinforcing our position as a leading player in tokenizing real-world assets. Many industry players approached us to learn more about how Tiamonds is reshaping the space, and the interest we received was a great affirmation of our vision.

Key Themes & Market Sentiment

The conversations at PBW centered around the growing interest in solving liquidity challenges in the RWA sector. We saw increasing demand from institutions eager to explore the potential of tokenized assets within DeFi applications, particularly around yield generation. The sentiment was clear: the future of tokenized, tangible assets is bright.

Memorable Moments

PBW also offered unforgettable experiences. A VIP dinner at the Louvre Museum, capped by a visit to the Mona Lisa, was truly a memorable moment. We closed the week with the iconic PBW party at the iconic Moulin Rouge, a perfect end to an impactful week.
(Image of Mona Lisa)

Paris Blockchain Week was a pivotal moment for Tiamonds. We gained significant traction, visibility, and inbound interest from leading blockchains, DeFi protocols, and institutional players. It has positioned us as a key player in the RWA tokenization space.

We’re taking this momentum with us to our next destination. See you at Token 2049 in Dubai!
