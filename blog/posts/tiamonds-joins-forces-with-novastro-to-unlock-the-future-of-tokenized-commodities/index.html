Tiamonds Joins Forces with Novastro to Unlock the Future of Tokenized Commodities

June 18, 2025 - 4 min read

Abstract / Summary

Tiamonds is excited to announce a strategic partnership with Novastro, a next-generation infrastructure platform designed for cross-chain, compliant real-world asset (RWA) tokenization. This partnership brings Tiamonds, a pioneer in tokenizing diamonds, gold, silver, and platinum, into Novastro’s issuer network, creating a powerful synergy between asset legitimacy and smart infrastructure.

Together, the two companies are building an ecosystem that enables the seamless and secure issuance, verification, and trading of tokenized commodities across multiple blockchain networks.

Key Highlights

  • Tiamonds becomes an official tokenized commodity issuer in the Novastro ecosystem.
  • Partnership enables compliant ERC-20 and ERC-721 token issuance across Ethereum, Arbitrum, Solana, and Sui.
  • SPV and DTC stack by Novastro supports a legally enforceable, secure issuance.
  • Tiamonds’ Total Tokenization (TOTO) model ensures full 1:1 backing and verifiability of each asset.
  • A shared vision to make luxury asset ownership accessible, transparent, and globally available.

Introducing the Partnership

Tiamonds has officially joined the Novastro ecosystem, a modular, cross-chain platform purpose-built for tokenizing and trading real-world assets with compliance and scalability at its core.

As a recognized issuer of tokenized luxury commodities, Tiamonds brings a robust catalog of real-world assets to Novastro’s growing network. Diamonds, gold, silver, and platinum, traditionally locked in exclusive, hard-to-access markets, are now available on-chain in a secure and verifiable manner.

This partnership bridges two core layers of the RWA stack: physical asset integrity and programmable asset mobility.

Opening Up Precious Commodities with Tiamonds

For centuries, precious metals and gemstones were reserved for a select few, with complex logistics, unclear pricing, and high entry barriers.

Tiamonds is changing that narrative with its Total Tokenization (TOTO) model, offering 1:1 backed tokens representing real assets stored in secure vaults in Liechtenstein. Every tokenized asset on the Tiamonds platform is:

  • Vault-custodied and fully insured
  • Authenticated and legally enforceable
  • Backed by regulatory partnerships, including LCX

This commitment to real-world assurance makes Tiamonds a natural fit for Novastro’s high-integrity infrastructure layer.

What This Means for Users

This collaboration delivers clear, tangible benefits to users seeking real-world value in digital finance:

  • Cross-chain mobility: Buy and trade tokenized assets across top blockchains.
  • Transparent issuance: Every token is backed by documentation, storage, and insurance.
  • Regulatory confidence: Compliance built into issuance and trading through Novastro’s SPVs.
  • Luxury, unlocked: Access real-world diamonds and metals like never before globally and securely.

Tiamonds and Novastro are building a frictionless bridge between traditional asset classes and modern Web3 infrastructure.

Looking Ahead

The partnership between Tiamonds and Novastro is a foundational step in redefining how real-world assets are owned, issued, and traded.

As Novastro transitions to mainnet and Tiamonds expands its asset catalog, users can expect even more integrations, financial products, and liquidity pathways. This is not just tokenization; it is the infrastructure for a new financial reality.

About Tiamonds

Tiamonds is the world’s first and largest diamond tokenization platform, now expanding its reach to gold, silver, sapphires, platinum, and other real-world assets. Built on the principle of freedom of ownership, Tiamonds offers secure, verifiable, and transparent access to luxury commodities through blockchain technology. Every asset is authenticated, insured, and stored in regulated vaults, offering users a new way to invest in tangible value with digital efficiency and security.

About Novastro

Novastro is a cross-chain, compliance-first infrastructure protocol designed for the tokenization and trading of real-world assets. Through its modular architecture, SPV structures, and decentralized compliance tools, Novastro enables scalable and auditable RWA issuance across top blockchain ecosystems. Built for asset issuers and DeFi builders alike, Novastro bridges the gap between off-chain value and on-chain innovation.
