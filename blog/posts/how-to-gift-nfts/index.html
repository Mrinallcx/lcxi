How to Gift NFTs?

#digitalassets #NFTs
January 4, 2024 - 5 min read

NFTs present an innovative and creative method to give meaningful and one-of-a-kind gifts. By using NFTs as a gift, someone can give someone a digital work of art, collectible, or virtual experience.  Culturally, the perception of gifts has transitioned.  There are numerous factors to consider prior to gifting. Traditional materials are perishable and depreciate over time. Due to physical dependencies, traditional ways of gifting have had to take a back seat during the pandemic, giving rise to the growing demand for digital assets in this post-pandemic era. NFTs and cryptocurrencies have emerged as gifting options. 

Each NFT is unique, symbolizing scarcity and exclusivity. Giving an NFT as a present displays thoughtfulness and the desire to discover something truly unique. Recipients can personalize NFTs based on their interests and passions. Whether interested in creative works, musical performances, sports, or virtual worlds, they can discover an NFT that precisely matches what they want. Also, the value of NFTs increases over time. By giving an NFT as a present, one gives the recipient the chance to not only appreciate the digital possession but also profit from its potential future value. 

How to Gift NFTs?

Setting up a digital wallet and transferring the NFT to the person who will receive it are two of the stages required to gift an NFT. 

Here is a detailed procedure: 

Setup a Digital Wallet: Create a digital wallet to receive NFTs as a gift. Select a wallet that is compatible with the blockchain network where the NFT resides. MetaMask for Ethereum-based NFTs, the most widely used and popular crypto wallet option. Configure and secure the wallet per the provider’s instructions. Gifting NFTs encourages the Digital Era with Unusual and notable Presents.

Buy the NFT: After setting up the wallet, one has to get the NFT they desire. Gift NFTs by  exploring NFT marketplaces in order to locate the desired NFT. Consider factors such as the work of art, its rarity, and the notoriety of the creator. Follow the instructions of the marketplace to acquire the NFT using digital currencies, typically Ether (ETH) for Ethereum-based NFTs. 

Send the NFT: After acquiring the NFT, one can transmit it to the specified receiver as a gift. The recipient’s wallet address is a distinctive identification number for their digital wallet. Identify an option for transferring or sending the NFT on their wallet’s interface or the marketplace. Enter the wallet address of the recipient and finalize the transaction. 

Ways to Gift NFTs:

For those who do not have a crypto wallet:

Fill their wallets: Transfer their preferred NFTs on the basis of their preferences. 

Gift NFTs based on their utility (such as music festival/event credentials along with NFTs). 

For those who do not have a crypto wallet:

Set up their metamask wallet (similar to establishing a young adult’s bank account) and assist them in acquiring their first NFT, or simply transfer one to them, and instruct them on how to list on the secondary market.

The numerous advantages of gifting NFTs surpass those of e-gifting. These benefits apply to both the donor and the recipient. In the process of purchasing the NFTs, the donor learns about the most recent technological advancement, namely blockchain technology. The recipient of the gift has now acquired a class of appreciating assets. In the event that he or she decides to sell the NFTs on the secondary market, he or she will receive everlasting royalties. Moreover, they possess the social media gloating rights to which they are entitled.

What Should You Know Before Gifting NFTs? 

Before giving an NFT as a gift, consider the following: 

  •  Confirm that the NFT matches the recipient’s interests and preferences. Consider the patron’s preferred artists, genres, and/or subjects when choosing the NFT.
  • If the recipient is unfamiliar with NFTs, give them knowledge about what they are, how to administer them, and the underlying blockchain technology. Assist them in understanding how to access and observe their gifted NFT. 
  • Before sending the NFT, double-check the recipient’s wallet address. Transferring the NFT to the incorrect address could result in irrevocable loss. 
  • There may be gas fees associated with the network of blockchain servers when sending an NFT. Gas fees cover the expense of transaction processing. Ensure that there is sufficient crypto in the designated wallet to pay for these fees.

Conclusion

The purpose of gifting an NFT is to communicate with people in interesting, and creative ways. In addition, if someone gift NFTs with value, the recipient may eventually benefit from the digital asset. There are a multitude of application scenarios for such kinds of digital credentials, each appealing to a different industry. Consider the type of individual they are purchasing for and select a non-financial gift that speaks to their values and interests.

It can be a thrilling and meaningful experience to give an NFT as a gift. NFT gifting has an opportunity to create a substantial alternative gifting industry on blockchain as Crypto and NFT adoption accelerate. The gifting NFTs has become an integral component of blockchain relationships.
